HOW TO PLAY

It's puzzle time, kids. Put the scrambled pieces in order and assemble the Avengers!

To start, look for the empty square. Next, click on a puzzle piece to move it into the empty square. Keep going until you complete the puzzle! If you need a little help, click the "Preview" button to see what the fully assembled puzzle should look like.

Use the arrows to scroll to the next puzzle.
